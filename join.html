Join Trinity Chambers

We are always looking for talented, ambitious barristers and pupils to join one of the North of England's leading chambers.

Pupillage at Trinity

Trinity Chambers offers a rewarding and challenging pupillage. We are committed to developing the next generation of barristers and invest significantly in training and support.

What We Offer

★

Competitive Award

Pupillage award well above the Bar Council minimum, reflecting our commitment to attracting the best candidates.

⚖

Broad Experience

Rotate across multiple practice areas during your first six, gaining invaluable exposure to a wide range of work.

✉

Dedicated Supervisors

Learn from senior barristers and King's Counsel with decades of experience across their specialist areas.

♬

Early Court Work

Second-six pupils accept their own instructions and appear in court, building confidence and experience rapidly.

How to Apply

1

Online Application

Submit your application through the Pupillage Gateway during the application window (typically January–February).

2

Written Exercise

Shortlisted candidates complete a written exercise testing legal reasoning and written communication.

3

First Interview

Invited candidates attend a structured competency-based interview with members of Chambers.

4

Final Interview

Final-stage candidates have a second interview, including a short advocacy exercise before a panel.

Applications for 2026/27 Pupillage

Applications open in January 2026 via the Pupillage Gateway. We typically offer two pupillages per year.
