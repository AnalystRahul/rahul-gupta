About Trinity Chambers

One of the North of England's leading barristers' chambers, providing expert legal advice and representation since 1970.

Who We Are

Trinity Chambers is one of the North of England's leading sets of barristers' chambers. With offices in Newcastle, Middlesbrough and Leeds, we provide expert legal advice and representation across a wide spectrum of practice areas.

Our barristers are regularly recommended in the Legal 500 and Chambers & Partners directories, and our silks deal with cases of the highest complexity across the UK and internationally.

We pride ourselves on being approachable, commercially astute and responsive. We offer the most appropriate and cost-effective barrister or solicitor advocate for your needs, ensuring every client receives the dedicated attention their matter deserves.

Our History

From our founding in Newcastle over five decades ago, Trinity Chambers has grown into one of the most respected sets on the Northern Circuit.

1970

Founded in Newcastle

Trinity Chambers established at The Custom House, Quayside, Newcastle upon Tyne, with a founding team of six barristers.

1985

Expansion to Middlesbrough

Opened our second set of chambers in Middlesbrough to serve clients across Teesside and the wider North East.

1998

First King's Counsel Appointments

Trinity's first barristers appointed King's Counsel, recognising our growing reputation for excellence in criminal and civil law.

2008

Leeds Office Opens

Expanded into Yorkshire with a new set of chambers in Leeds, bringing our services to a broader client base.

2015

Legal 500 Top-Tier Recognition

Achieved top-tier ranking across multiple practice areas in both the Legal 500 and Chambers & Partners directories.

2025

Over 100 Barristers

Trinity Chambers today comprises over 100 barristers and silks, making us one of the largest sets on the Northern Circuit.
